Crusade

The contents of the Cutthroat and Challenger Leagues have been updated. In February there was a poll in the forum presenting a variety of different options. We’re currently in discussions with Bluehole in regards to when we’ll apply the result of this poll to our region. The content within the first month of the Crusade will be as follows::

  • Cutthroat League
  • Corsairs' Stronghold
  • Gridiron
  • Fraywind Canyon
  • Champions' Skyring
  • Challenger League
  • Forsaken Island (Hard) [High Score]
  • Dreadspire [High Score]
  • Sky Cruiser Endeavor (Hard) [Accumulated Score]
  • Bathysmal Rise (Hard) [Accumulated Score]

In addition, the rewards have been adjusted to match the list in the forum. Previously, our customer support team had to manually award the additional 1500 Masterwork Alkahest to the top placed players. From now on the full amount of Masterwork Alkahest will be sent to the guild master per in-game mail.
