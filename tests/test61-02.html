Class Changes

Valkyrie

  • Shining Crescent
    • Reduced crit damage by 10%
  • Runeburst
    • Reduced crit damage by 10%
  • Glaive Strike
    • Reduced crit damage by 10%
  • Bloodflower
    • Reduced the duration of stun on players and monsters to 3 sec.
  • Backstab
    • Removed stagger effect on players and monsters
    • Added immobility to players and monsters
  • Moon Blessing
    • Glyph of Restoration: Reduced duration from 12 sec. to 6 sec.
    • Glyph of Restoration: Change to 2 glyph points

Warrior

  • Cross Parry
    • The cooldown of Scythe is reduced by 0.5 sec. when successfully blocking monster attacks.
    • The effect of Tenaciousness is only activated when successfully blocking monster attacks.
    • 1 stack is added when successfully blocking monster attacks.
  • Traverse Cut X
    • The attack speed of up to 5 party members is increased when Traverse Cut X is used in Defensive Stance II.
    • Changed the icon type of the effect of Traverse Cut to skill.
  • Combative Strike XI
    • If Combative Strike XI is used against a monster in Defensive Stance II, the target's endurance is reduced by 10%.
  • Poison Blade
    • Green infection effect was removed.

Reaper

  • Soul Reversal
    • Added 2.5 sec. stun effect
    • Soul Reversal can now be used against boss monsters and can be used repeatedly with other Reapers.
  • Shrouded Escape
    • Cooldown changed to 1 min. 30 sec.
  • Smite
    • Noctenium is not used
    • No collision with party members
  • Grim Strike
    • Cooldown changed to 5 sec.
  • Death Spiral
    • Noctenium is not used
  • Veil of Shadows
    • Cooldown changed to 1 hour
  • Shadow Reaping Reinforcement
    • Added new passive skill that reinforces Shadow Reaping
    • When Shadow Reaping is active, attack speed increases up to 5 times by 5% per hit on monsters.
    • Cannot be stacked with the effect of Lein's Dark Root Beer.
    • The duration is not shown with the effect is stacked. The stacking effect disappears at the end of Shadow Reaping.
  • Cable Step
    • Glyph of the Swift: Recharge speed of the skill Cable Step is increased by 50% for 2.5 sec. if the skill hits successfully.
  • Shadow Burst
    • Powerlinked Cable Step: Added glyph that increases the probability of crits.
  • Whipsaw
    • Glyph of Power [Uncommon]: Skill damage is only increased when monsters are targeted.
    • Glyph of Power [Rare]: Skill damage is only increased when monsters are targeted.

Lancer
